About Us

This wasn't born in a boardroom. You won't find its origin story in spreadsheets or market projections, and it was never a soulless PowerPoint presentation.

It started on a quiet hiking trail, somewhere deep in the lush, damp forests of British Columbia. It started with the feeling of real earth under our boots and a shared, unspoken realization that something vital in our modern lives had simply gone missing. We were doing everything we were supposed to do. We were chasing deadlines, climbing corporate ladders, and scrolling through endless, numbing feeds until our thumbs were sore. But we felt… disconnected. Disconnected from the natural world, from each other, and, most terrifyingly, from ourselves.

That’s where the magic began for us. Our own personal journeys with psilocybin, specifically with the profound, gentle power of microdosing, changed everything. It wasn’t about escaping reality; it was about finally finding our way back to it. This wasn't some wild, reality-bending trip. It was quieter. Gentler. It was the constant, nagging noise in our heads finally turning down a notch on a chaotic Monday morning. It was the sudden surge of creative energy that broke through a project that had felt stale for months. It was the rediscovery of wonder, of awe, in a world that had started to feel grey, transactional, and small. We had found something that helped us quiet the external noise and amplify the quiet, essential signal of our own consciousness.

But as we explored this new inner landscape, we hit a wall. The space felt secretive, inconsistent, and it completely lacked the kind of care and intention we believed this profound medicine deserved. It was a world of anonymous online sellers, questionable products, and a total lack of guidance. It honestly pissed us off. We knew we couldn’t be the only Canadians feeling this way—curious, hopeful, but looking for a safe, transparent, and trustworthy guide for the journey.

So, Go Micro Magic was born. It was born from a deep, almost obsessive passion for wellness and a profound respect for the ancient wisdom of fungi. We connected with dedicated, small-batch growers across Canada who share our ethos—people who understand that the real magic isn’t just in the psilocybin molecule, but in the quality, the care, and the human intention that is infused into every single step of the process.

This is why we don’t see ourselves as just an online store. We’re more like stewards for a sacred experience. Every single product we offer, from our precisely measured microdose capsules to our thoughtfully crafted edibles, is a tool that we have used and deeply believe in ourselves. This is more than a business to us; it’s our community. It’s our way of sharing the clarity and connection we were so lucky to find.

Look, if you’re here, you’re probably a little curious. Maybe even a little skeptical. We get it. Maybe you’re seeking a bit more creativity, a little less anxiety, or just a new perspective on things. Whatever your path, whatever your reason, we’re just glad you found your way to ours.

Welcome to the magic.
